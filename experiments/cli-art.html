I have a feeling this kind of thing is very common amongst developers. Code something in one language and then see what it looks like in another.

I wanted to use the terminal window for random generative art

It's not amazing art, but basically, you plug in 4 characters and press enter

Artwork is then generated based on a random distribution of those characters

I did Javascript first. Then Python, C++ and finally Rust
